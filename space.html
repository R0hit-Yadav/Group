History Talk

Aryabhata (1975) : India's first satellite, Aryabhata, was launched on April 19, 1975, by the Soviet Union.
SLV (1980-1994) : India's first experimental satellite launch vehicle (SLV) was developed by the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
Mangalyaan (2013) : Mangalyaan, also known as the Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), was launched on November 5, 2013. It made India the first Asian country to reach Martian orbit and the fourth space agency in the world to do so.
Chandrayaan-2 (2019) : Chandrayaan-2 was India's second lunar exploration mission launched on July 22, 2019. While the lander Vikram lost communication during its descent, the orbiter remains operational and continues to provide valuable data.
